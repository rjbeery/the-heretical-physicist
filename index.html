Research focus

Current work examines the causal structure near putative event horizons, including scenarios in which infalling signals do not return and cases with semiclassical evaporation modeled in ingoing Vaidya coordinates.

Publications

The Heretical Physicist: Revisiting Einstein's Objection to the Event Horizon

Reports in Advances of Physical Sciences, Vol. 9 (2025), Open Access.

Revisits Einstein’s objection to event horizons using causal arguments, including an inelastic-rope thought experiment formulated to test operational continuity near a null surface.

Read on World Scientific

No Passing Zone (Redux): Horizon Chasing in Evaporating Black Holes via Ingoing Vaidya Coordinates

Reports in Advances of Physical Sciences, Vol. 9 (2025), Open Access.

Analyzes timelike infall in evaporating spacetimes using ingoing Vaidya coordinates, with attention to horizon behavior under mass loss.
